FAQ

My trip was cancelled — what should I do first?

Save evidence (emails/screenshots), check “Manage booking” options, and keep receipts for extra costs. Then decide whether to rebook or switch destinations based on your dates.

Flights cancelled — should I cancel everything?

Not necessarily. If your vacation dates are fixed, switching to a connected destination can save the holiday. Keep your itinerary light until routes are confirmed.

What is the best kind of Plan B destination?

Choose a transport hub city with airports, rail links and simple public transport. It reduces friction and makes re-planning easier.

Where should I stay in Milan for easy logistics?

Near Milano Centrale is ideal for airport transfers, trains for day trips, and metro/trams across the city — perfect for last-minute changes.
