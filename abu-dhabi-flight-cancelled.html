Fast checklist (15 minutes)

1) Save evidence

Keep the cancellation email/screenshot, booking number, and ticket number.

2) Check rebooking

Use “Manage booking” first (often faster than calling). Keep reference numbers.

3) Keep receipts

If you pay for extra transport or accommodation, keep receipts for insurance/claims.

FAQ

Abu Dhabi flight cancelled — what should I do first?

Save the cancellation notice and your booking details, then check rebooking options and your insurance conditions. Keep reference numbers and receipts.

If my UAE plans changed, what is a good alternative destination?

A connected hub city is usually best. Milan offers airports, rail and metro, plus lots of culture and easy day trips — so you can rebuild your holiday quickly.

Where should I stay in Milan for easy airport and train access?

Near Milano Centrale. It’s the simplest base for airport transfers, trains, and metro lines across the city.

What can I do in Milan in 3–5 days?

A balanced plan: Duomo + Galleria + Brera, Castello + parks, Navigli evening — and one optional day trip (Lake Como, Bergamo, Verona).
