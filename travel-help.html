Checklist

1) Save evidence

  • Screenshot/email of cancellation
  • Booking + ticket numbers
  • Receipts for extra costs

2) Rebooking

  • Try “Manage booking” first
  • Check nearby airports if reasonable
  • Re-check baggage rules

3) Insurance

  • Open a claim early if needed
  • Ask required documents
  • Keep reference numbers

Planning principle: hub city + comfortable base + light itinerary = less stress and more control.
